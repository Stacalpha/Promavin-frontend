  Promavin produces perfectly fitting bespoke menswear with zero delays in delivery.

When it comes to clothing, fit is everything. This, in addition to quality and delivery timeliness are the most important things to us at Promavin.

  Promavin Couture is a start-up in the Nigerian fashion industry with a mission to solve the problem of poor service delivery that plagues the tailoring industry in Nigeria. The 'home service' nature of our process also offers customers maximum convenience in accessing our service.

  Promavin is a male clothing brand currently operating only in parts of Lagos Mainland. We're working to expand our services to more locations throughout Nigeria in the near future.
